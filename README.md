### About Me

I'm a Software Engineer and AI Engineer that loves building software with AI at its core. I believe this is the software of the future and the one that solves real problems today.

I've worked across very different worlds: banking apps (ImaginBank, CaixaBank, Banc Sabadell),
innovative products for seniors at a Swiss startup, and now my own company.

When I first saw ChatGPT in 2022, something clicked. AI wasn't new, but for the first time it was in everyone's hands. I knew I had to build something.

That became WeUp an AI platform that helps marketing agencies manage LinkedIn content for their clients. Today, 500+ companies use it across multiple countries.
